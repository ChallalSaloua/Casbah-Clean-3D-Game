# 🎮 Casbah Clean

A 3D educational serious game developed with Unity and Blender to promote environmental awareness and preserve the cultural heritage of the Casbah of Algiers.

---

## 📖 Overview

Casbah Clean is an educational adventure game where players take on the role of Mokrane, a university student committed to protecting his community and preserving one of Algeria's most iconic historical sites.

Through a series of interactive levels, players collect waste, sort recyclable materials, overcome environmental obstacles, and contribute to the restoration of polluted urban spaces. The project combines entertainment and education to encourage responsible environmental behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Raise awareness about environmental pollution.
- Encourage recycling and waste sorting.
- Promote civic responsibility.
- Highlight the importance of preserving cultural heritage.
- Demonstrate how small actions can have a positive impact on society.

---

## 🕹 Gameplay Features

- Waste collection system
- Recycling mechanics
- Environmental challenges and obstacles
- Score and energy management
- Progressive level progression
- Educational storytelling
- Interactive missions

---

## 🏛 Game Setting

The game takes place in the historic Casbah of Algiers, where players explore streets, houses, and green areas affected by pollution.

As the game progresses, players can observe the positive transformation of both the environment and Mokrane's personal house, reinforcing the impact of sustainable actions.
